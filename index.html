About Our Restaurant

Si Señor, established in 2013, is a lively and authentic family-owned Mexican restaurant in St. Mary parish. Owner Enrique Loza has been serving the community for decades by filling both stomachs and hearts. Since a young age, Enrique served in the restaurant industry, building a name for himself as the kind and caring figure that customers could rely on. Beyond the food, Enrique would engage with the community by coaching the youth as a soccer coach. Now, he partners with other local businesses to fundraise and stimulate the economy to help grow our beautiful parish into something even better. Don't take our word for it, ask anyone in town about the Loza family and everyone will tell you exactly how kind, caring, and loving this family is. Or come in and see for yourself!
